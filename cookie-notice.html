Cookie Notice

Last updated: June 12, 2026

CYBERDYNE uses only the cookies and storage needed to run the Service. We do not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies, and our analytics are cookieless.

1. What we use

  • Strictly necessary — sign-in. A session/authentication cookie (set by our auth provider, Supabase, and during Sign-In with Ethereum) keeps you logged in. Without it, you can’t use the app.
  • Strictly necessary — security. Cloudflare sets short-lived cookies (e.g., __cf_bm) for bot management and abuse protection.
  • Analytics — cookieless. We use Cloudflare Web Analytics to measure aggregate page views and performance. It does not set cookies, does not track you across sites, and does not build a profile of you.

2. What we don’t use

No advertising cookies, no third-party ad/tracking networks, no cross-site profiling.

3. Managing cookies

You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings. Note that disabling the strictly-necessary cookies above will prevent you from signing in and using the app.

4. Changes & contact

We may update this Notice and will revise the date above. Questions: serafino@cyberdyne-os.xyz. See also our Privacy Policy.
